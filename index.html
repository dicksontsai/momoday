S0mewhat Underc00ked

This is the game that Ben made to propose to Sarah, and now you can play it too (ideally with a 2nd person)!

The game is inspired by 0verc00ked, which Ben and Sarah really enjoyed playing together (getting 3 ⭐ on all levels, naturally). The goal of the game is to cook jambalaya, one of their favourite things to cook together. It also features two of Ben and Sarah's favourite characters, Brown and Cony from LIN3 FRI3NDS.

Special thanks to Calvin Jee for composing the amazing music for the game!

Instructions

Playing as Brown or Cony, complete the orders that appear in the top left corner. You will need:

  • Rice (the white thing, it's hard to model rice)
  • Chopped green pepper
  • Chopped and fried chicken and/or sausage, depending on the order

These ingredients should be put into the pot together and served on a plate to the delivery window once fully cooked.

Controls

Brown (the bear)

  • Movement: WASD
  • Boost: Space
  • Pick up/drop: E
  • Chop: Left Shift

Cony (the rabbit)

  • Movement: Arrow keys
  • Boost: Enter
  • Pick up/drop: /
  • Chop: Right Shift
